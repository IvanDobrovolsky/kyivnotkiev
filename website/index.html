Key Findings

Category hierarchy matters. Geographical names adopted fastest (Kharkiv: 92%), food terms barely moved (Borshch: ~8%), and "disaster brands" like Chernobyl are nearly immovable (3%).

Social media leads traditional media. Reddit communities adopted Ukrainian spellings 5‚Äď9x faster than news outlets for every pair measured.

Two catalysts. The 2018 #KyivNotKiev campaign and the 2022 full-scale invasion each triggered measurable adoption spikes, but their effects varied dramatically by category.

The holdout list. Hundreds of media domains still predominantly use Russian-origin spellings. This data informs policy recommendations for Ukraine's MFA.

About

This is the world's largest computational study of real-time toponymic change. We track how 69 Ukrainian names are spelled across 7 independent data sources spanning two decades.

The pipeline is fully open source, reproducible with a single command (make reproduce), and runs on Google Cloud Platform using Apache Spark for TB-scale processing.
