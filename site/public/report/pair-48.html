Summary

67.9% adoption across 649.9K mentions (95% CI: [97.5%, 97.7%]). Wikipedia leads at 98%, while Reddit lags at 38%.

Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2025: 92 mentions (7x volume spike), 91% adoption; YouTube 2022: 21 mentions (3x volume spike), 67% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Gopak" distinctively co-occurs with fair, youtube, provided, piano; "Hopak" clusters with day, dance, ukrainian, combat. These patterns reveal how each spelling form lives in distinct discourse communities.

Context matters: "Hopak" dominates in culture arts contexts (52% UA share), while "Gopak" persists strongest in general news (67% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 19 percentage points.

AI models show 94% alignment (TAS) with 100% spontaneous generation — a 10pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (68%) with mainstream momentum. remaining holdouts in Reddit likely reflect legacy content rather than active resistance.
