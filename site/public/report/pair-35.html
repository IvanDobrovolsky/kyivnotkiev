Summary

43.6% adoption across 17.9M mentions (95% CI: [49.9%, 50.0%]). Reddit leads at 58%, while Google Trends lags at 9%.

Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 70 mentions (12x volume spike), 83% adoption; YouTube 2011: 37 mentions (3x volume spike), 54% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Collocation analysis (PMI-ranked): "Kievan Rus" distinctively co-occurs with mongol, medieval, slavic, empire; "Kyivan Rus" clusters with kyivan, history, russia, ukraine; both forms share kievan, byzantine, rus. These patterns reveal how each spelling form lives in distinct discourse communities.

Context matters: "Kyivan Rus" dominates in culture arts contexts (80% UA share), while "Kievan Rus" persists strongest in religion (74% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 55 percentage points.

AI models show 43% alignment (TAS) with 21% spontaneous generation — a 51pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia.

Recommendation: Adoption is progressing but contested (44%). Google Trends (9%) represents the biggest opportunity for improvement. LLM training data updates would increase AI alignment from current 21% free recall.
