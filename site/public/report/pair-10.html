Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 360 mentions (5x volume spike), 29% adoption; Reddit 2024: 145 mentions (2x volume spike), 52% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Chernobyl" is most strongly associated with accident, fallout, thyroid (z=22); "Chornobyl" is distinguished by ukraine, ukrainian, heart.

Context matters: "Chornobyl" dominates in culture arts contexts (83% UA share), while "Chernobyl" persists strongest in history (58% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 41 percentage points.

Recommendation: Adoption of "Chornobyl" remains very low (24%). institutional endorsement and media style guide adoption would be the most effective levers. LLM training data updates would increase AI alignment from current 0% free recall.
