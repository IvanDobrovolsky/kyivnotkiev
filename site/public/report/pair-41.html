Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 66 mentions (7x volume spike), 82% adoption; YouTube 2016: -31pp adoption shift (75 mentions). These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Cherkassy" is most strongly associated with church, patriarch, orthodox (z=16); "Cherkasy" is distinguished by region, study, research.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (84%) with mainstream momentum. remaining holdouts in Google Ngrams likely reflect legacy content rather than active resistance.
