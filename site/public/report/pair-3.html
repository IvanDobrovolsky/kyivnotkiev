Summary

39.9% adoption across 10.1M mentions (95% CI: [64.8%, 65.0%]). Wikipedia leads at 80%, while Google Ngrams lags at 3%.

Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 336 mentions (13x volume spike), 38% adoption; Reddit 2014: 88 mentions (6x volume spike), 6% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Odessa" distinctively co-occurs with church, strong, orthodox, jewish; "Odesa" clusters with ukraine, russia, russian, missile. These patterns reveal how each spelling form lives in distinct discourse communities.

Context matters: "Odesa" dominates in war conflict contexts (79% UA share), while "Odessa" persists strongest in history (77% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 56 percentage points.

AI models show 46% alignment (TAS) with 24% spontaneous generation — a 53pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia. Moscow Patriarchate maintains 99% preference for "Odessa" — institutional resistance.

Recommendation: Adoption is progressing but contested (40%). Google Ngrams (3%) represents the biggest opportunity for improvement. LLM training data updates would increase AI alignment from current 24% free recall.
