Summary

55.4% adoption across 14.4M mentions (95% CI: [48.5%, 48.7%]). YouTube leads at 59%, while GDELT News lags at 31%.

Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 368 mentions (8x volume spike), 51% adoption; Reddit 2025: 118 mentions (2x volume spike), 70% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Collocation analysis (PMI-ranked): "Donbass" distinctively co-occurs with donbass, die, der, ore; "Donbas" clusters with donbas, conflict, nie, crimea; both forms share mines, coal, mine. These patterns reveal how each spelling form lives in distinct discourse communities.

Context matters: "Donbas" dominates in war conflict contexts (55% UA share), while "Donbass" persists strongest in history (64% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 19 percentage points.

AI models show 92% alignment (TAS) with 99% spontaneous generation — a 13pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia.

Recommendation: Adoption is progressing but contested (55%). GDELT News (31%) represents the biggest opportunity for improvement.
