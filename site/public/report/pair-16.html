Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2025: -22pp adoption shift (63 mentions); YouTube 2016: 65 mentions (3x volume spike), 14% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Dniester" is most strongly associated with water, river, moldova (z=9); "Dnister" is distinguished by lviv, place, ukrainian.
