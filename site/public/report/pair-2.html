Summary

79.1% adoption across 7.3M mentions (95% CI: [95.2%, 95.3%]). GDELT News leads at 98%, while Google Ngrams lags at 28%.

Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 356 mentions (13x volume spike), 61% adoption; Reddit 2014: 70 mentions (7x volume spike), 14% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Kharkov" is most strongly associated with church, orthodox, kursk (z=28); "Kharkiv" is distinguished by study, analysis, art.

Context matters: "Kharkiv" dominates in academic science contexts (62% UA share), while "Kharkov" persists strongest in history (72% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 34 percentage points.

AI models show 96% alignment (TAS) with 98% spontaneous generation — a 1pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia. Moscow Patriarchate maintains 95% preference for "Kharkov" — institutional resistance.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (79%) with mainstream momentum. remaining holdouts in Google Ngrams likely reflect legacy content rather than active resistance.
