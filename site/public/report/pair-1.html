Summary

64.4% adoption across 30.7M mentions (95% CI: [52.4%, 52.5%]). Wikipedia leads at 86%, while Google Ngrams lags at 23%.

Volume spikes detected: Reddit 2022: 482 mentions (4x volume spike), 51% adoption; YouTube 2023: 209 mentions (2x volume spike), 53% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Collocation analysis (PMI-ranked): "Kiev" distinctively co-occurs with kiev, church, patriarch, the; "Kyiv" clusters with kyiv, grid, agglomeration, map. These patterns reveal how each spelling form lives in distinct discourse communities.

Context matters: "Kyiv" dominates in academic science contexts (64% UA share), while "Kiev" persists strongest in religion (87% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 51 percentage points.

AI models show 91% alignment (TAS) with 88% spontaneous generation — a 8pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia. Moscow Patriarchate maintains 97% preference for "Kiev" — institutional resistance.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (64%) with mainstream momentum. remaining holdouts in Reddit, GDELT News likely reflect legacy content rather than active resistance. LLM training data updates would increase AI alignment from current 88% free recall.
