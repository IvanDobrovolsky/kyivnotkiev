Summary

85.3% adoption across 18.9K mentions (95% CI: [80.5%, 83.5%]). Google Trends leads at 95%, while YouTube lags at 60%.

Volume spikes detected: YouTube 2019: 53 mentions (3x volume spike), 45% adoption; YouTube 2023: 39 mentions (2x volume spike), 59% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Kiev National University" is most strongly associated with ukraine, shevchenko, taras (z=13); "Kyiv National University" is distinguished by educational, research, law.

Context matters: "Kyiv National University" dominates in academic science contexts (83% UA share), while "Kiev National University" persists strongest in travel tourism (77% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 60 percentage points.

AI models show 96% alignment (TAS) with 87% spontaneous generation — a 8pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (85%) with mainstream momentum. LLM training data updates would increase AI alignment from current 87% free recall.
