Summary

84.6% adoption across 7.7M mentions (95% CI: [97.0%, 97.1%]). GDELT News leads at 98%, while Google Ngrams lags at 50%.

Volume spikes detected: YouTube 2022: -21pp adoption shift (106 mentions); Reddit 2022: 175 mentions (5x volume spike), 86% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Lvov" is most strongly associated with warsaw, church, school (z=26); "Lviv" is distinguished by region, development, study.

Context matters: "Lviv" dominates in academic science contexts (66% UA share), while "Lvov" persists strongest in general news (76% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 42 percentage points.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (85%) with mainstream momentum.
