64.0% adoption across 1.3M mentions (95% CI: [87.4%, 87.8%]). Wikipedia leads at 99%, while GDELT News lags at 2%.

Volume spikes detected: YouTube 2023: 109 mentions (2x volume spike), 56% adoption; Reddit 2022: 61 mentions (3x volume spike), 57% adoption. These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Contrastive analysis (log-odds ratio): "Uzhgorod" is most strongly associated with church, orthodox, patriarch (z=11); "Uzhhorod" is distinguished by hungarian, strong, government.

Context matters: "Uzhhorod" dominates in politics contexts (65% UA share), while "Uzhgorod" persists strongest in travel tourism (62% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 27 percentage points.

AI models show 97% alignment (TAS) with 100% spontaneous generation — a 4pp recognition-recall gap indicating pre-training corpus inertia.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (64%) with mainstream momentum. remaining holdouts in GDELT News likely reflect legacy content rather than active resistance.
