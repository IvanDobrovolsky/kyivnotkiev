Volume spikes detected: YouTube 2023: 376 mentions (5x volume spike), 24% adoption; YouTube 2024: +40pp adoption shift (71 mentions). These correlate with major cultural or geopolitical events that drive public discourse and spelling adoption.

Collocation analysis (PMI-ranked): "Kiev ballet" distinctively co-occurs with gala, shevchenko, kyiv, ukrainian; "Kyiv ballet" clusters with swan, dancers, lake, company; both forms share opera, grand, national. These patterns reveal how each spelling form lives in distinct discourse communities.

Context matters: "Kyiv ballet" dominates in travel tourism contexts (100% UA share), while "Kiev ballet" persists strongest in culture arts (58% RU share). Domain-specific adoption varies by up to 58 percentage points.

Recommendation: Adoption is strong (72%) with mainstream momentum. remaining holdouts in Open Library Books, YouTube likely reflect legacy content rather than active resistance. LLM training data updates would increase AI alignment from current 89% free recall.
